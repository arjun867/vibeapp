About Us

Welcome to VIBE, a web app designed to help you make new friends who truly match your vibe.

We're a small but passionate team, led by Sammengi Arjun, a second-year student at IIEST, Shibpur. Arjun is dedicated to creating value-driven products with simple, user-friendly designs. He's assisted by two AI chatbots ChatGPT and Gemini, which provides invaluable support in developing the best possible app for you.

At VIBE, we believe that having friends who match your vibe can significantly enhance your life. Our app is designed to be clutter-free and easy to use, making it simple for you to connect with like-minded individuals. We're currently in our first version, but we're always listening to your feedback and working to improve. Stay tuned for exciting new features!

Our mission is to help you find friends who truly resonate with you. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at sammengiarjun@gmail.com. Your input is invaluable to us!
